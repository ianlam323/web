The diagram indicates the process of recycling a plastic bottle and how it is processed.

Overall, there are 9 main steps for recycling. The process is seperated in 2 main steps, collecting and processing.

Firstly, after using the plastic bottle is used. User should throw it in a desiginated recycling bin for collection. Then a driver will come and collect the recycled bootles and will sent to factories for further processing.

Afterwards, the bottles will be sorted manually so the unwanted bottles are filtered off. The process continous by compressing the high quality bottles into blocks using a compressor. Then, the bottles are being crushed and washed so that the plastic can be put into a machine to form plastic pellects. By heating the pallets, it will be formed into piles of raw material. The raw material are used to make products such as containers, clothings, and stationary. Finally, these products will be sold to public so that we can reuse it once again.
