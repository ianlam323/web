On the other hand, everyone should have their right in to choose where they want to work in, and should not be limited in staying in a certain place.

The UK healthcare system is National Healthcare System while Hong Kong has its own public healthcare system. A classic example is the large proportion of people who trains in UK and will get back to their country to work. Therefore in a macroeconomics view, people would like trained professionals to stay in where they did their training.

However, some people argue that everyone should have the basic human right to work or go wherever they want. If a person is limited to a place where their family is not with them, it would violate their human rights. Although the healthcare system around the world migth differ, their skills which they got from training are highly similar. In other words, it will only take a short period of time to adapt to the new envirioment as doctors are still doing their job, diagnosing patient. Therefore their skill will not be wasted.
